1
Watch Juno zoom past Jupiter.
请观赏朱诺号掠过木星所见到的景观。
2
NASA’s robotic spacecraft Juno is continuing on its now month-long, highly-elongated orbits around our Solar System’s largest planet.
朱诺号是NASA的无人航天器，现在正在一个又扁又长的轨道上，以月为周期环绕太阳系中最大的行星运行。
3
The featured video is from perijove 16, the sixteenth time that Juno passed near Jupiter since it arrived in mid-2016.
这段影片拍摄于近木轨道16。这是朱诺号从2016年接近木星以来，第十六次接近这颗行星。
4
Each perijove passes near a slightly different part of Jupiter’s cloud tops.
在每个近木轨道上，朱诺号会从不同位置掠过木星的外层大气。
5
This color-enhanced video has been digitally composed from 21 JunoCam still images, resulting in a 125-fold time-lapse.
这是一段1比125的延时影片，是由朱诺号相机拍摄的21张照片拼接而成的。
6
The video begins with Jupiter rising as Juno approaches from the north.
在影片的开头，朱诺号从北方接近木星，木星随之升起。
7
As Juno reaches its closest view — from about 3,500 kilometers over Jupiter’s cloud tops — the spacecraft captures the great planet in tremendous detail.
在距离木星云层顶端还有大约三千五百公里时，朱诺号到达了近木点，并在此捕捉到了这颗巨大行星的清晰影像。
8
Juno passes light zones and dark belts of clouds that circle the planet, as well as numerous swirling circular storms, many of which are larger than hurricanes on Earth.
朱诺号掠过了明暗相间、环绕着木星的云带，也掠过了许多圆形风暴——有些风暴比地球上的飓风还要大。
9
As Juno moves away, the remarkable dolphin-shaped cloud is visible.
随着朱诺号的远离，引人注目的海豚云出现了。
10
After the perijove, Jupiter recedes into the distance, now displaying the unusual clouds that appear over Jupiter’s south.
在离开近木轨道后，木星在视野中逐渐远去，而南极附近不同寻常的云系则变得清晰可见。
11
To get desired science data, Juno swoops so close to Jupiter that its instruments are exposed to very high levels of radiation.
为了获取所需的科学数据，朱诺号需要以非常低的高度掠过木星，航天器上的仪器也因此暴露在极高的辐射中。
